Digest KeyBill TextResolved by the Assembly of the State of California, That during the organizational recess, no bill shall be placed on the Desk for introduction except by a person designated by the author in writing. The designation shall be filed with the Chief Clerk; and be it furtherResolved, That the Committee on Rules, the Chief Clerk, or the Sergeant at Arms is authorized to receive from the Controller all warrants for the payment of Members, officers, and attachés of the Assembly; and be it furtherResolved, That the Chief Clerk shall provide copies of the Assembly Weekly Histories to the Legislative Counsel, Governor, Attorney General, and accredited press representatives.___________________ CORRECTIONS: Heading—Line 1.___________________
