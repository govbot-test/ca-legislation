[ Filed with Secretary of State July 10, 2025. ] LEGISLATIVE COUNSEL'S DIGESTACR 92, Mark González. Electronic Dance Music Month.This measure would declare the month of June 2025 as Electronic Dance Music Month.Digest Key Fiscal Committee: NO  Bill TextWHEREAS, Electronic dance music, or “EDM,” traces its roots back to the 1970s with the rise of disco and the innovative use of synthesizers in popular music; andWHEREAS, EDM encompasses a wide array of subgenres, including, but not limited to, disco, synthpop, techno, house, trance, drum and bass, dubstep, trap, hardstyle, and many more; andWHEREAS, EDM is a vibrant and inclusive musical form that brings people of all backgrounds together on the dance floor; andWHEREAS, EDM concerts are dynamic gatherings where the music, lights, and community come together in celebration; andWHEREAS, Attendees of these events are the heart of the EDM movement, and are known for their passion, creativity, and expressive fashion; andWHEREAS, EDM culture is rooted in the core values of Peace, Love, Unity, and Respect, commonly abbreviated as “PLUR”; andWHEREAS, Peace represents the elimination of conflict and the embrace of harmony; andWHEREAS, Love is shared through acts of kindness and goodwill; andWHEREAS, Unity invites all people into the community regardless of background, identity, or differences; andWHEREAS, Respect is shown through empathy, acceptance, and treating others as one wishes to be treated; andWHEREAS, These values reflect the spirit of California, a state that celebrates diversity, inclusion, and creative expression; andWHEREAS, California is home to some of the world’s most iconic EDM festivals, hosted in cities across the state, including the Cities of Los Angeles, San Francisco, San Diego, San Jose, San Bernardino, Bakersfield, Palm Springs, Coachella, and across the North Coast region; andWHEREAS, These festivals not only serve as cultural landmarks, but also generate millions in economic impact for local communities and the state; now, therefore, be itResolved by the Assembly of the State of California, the Senate thereof concurring, That the Legislature hereby recognizes the profound cultural, social, and economic impact of EDM on the lives of Californians; and be it furtherResolved, That the Legislature hereby declares the month of June 2025 as Electronic Dance Music Month; and be it furtherResolved, That the Chief Clerk of the Assembly transmit copies of this resolution to the author for appropriate distribution.
