Short-Term Rental Facilitator Act of 2025.Existing law, the Short-Term Rental Facilitator Act of 2025, imposes various requirements upon short-term rental facilitators, as defined.This bill would make nonsubstantive changes to the provision that names this Act.Digest Key Vote: MAJORITY Appropriation: NO Fiscal Committee: NO Local Program: NO  Bill TextThe people of the State of California do enact as follows:SECTION 1. Section 50990 of the Government Code is amended to read:50990. (a) This chapter shall be known, known and may be cited, cited as the “Short-Term Rental Facilitator Act of 2025.”(b) This chapter shall only apply to a local agency that adopts an ordinance that makes the provisions of this chapter applicable within its jurisdictional boundaries.
